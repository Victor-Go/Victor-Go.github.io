Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how our "Markdown Resume PDF Generator" (the "Application") handles your personal data. Resume content is processed in your browser and is not sent to our servers. We also use Google Analytics to understand aggregate site usage, as described below.

1. Information We Access and How We Use It

The Application integrates with Google Drive API to allow you to sync and back up your resume configuration files across devices. To achieve this, we request the following Google OAuth scope:

  • .../auth/drive.appdata: This permission allows the Application to store and manage resume backups only in its private Google Drive application-data folder. We do not have access to your other Google Drive files.

2. Local Storage and Direct Synchronization

All data processing happens entirely within your web browser (client-side):

  • Your resume data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage.
  • When you sync with Google Drive, the data is transmitted directly from your browser to Google's servers. We do not run any backend server, and we have no access to your Google credentials, access tokens, or resume files.

3. Third-Party Services

The Application uses the official Google Identity Services SDK to handle authentication. Your interaction with Google's login and authorization screens is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

We use Google Analytics 4 to measure aggregate usage, such as page views and language selection. Google Analytics may process technical information such as your IP address, browser, device, and approximate location in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. Google Analytics is configured with IP anonymization. You can prevent Google Analytics collection by using browser privacy controls or Google's opt-out browser add-on.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Since we do not store any of your data on our servers, we cannot delete it for you. You can completely remove your data at any time by:

  • Clearing your browser's local storage/cache.
  • Disconnecting Google Drive and clearing your local browser storage.
  • Removing the Application from your Google account through the Google Account Permissions page. Application-data files are stored in a private folder and are not visible as regular Google Drive files.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us via GitHub or our official website.
